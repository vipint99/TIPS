To rebuild from source rpm  --> but it will remove all existing .spec from SPEC/ and all source,patches from SOURCES

rpmbuild --rebuild --noclean ./SRPMS/pcsc-lite-1.8.8-8.el7.src.rpm


I could get all my .spec, source, Patches by this simple command

$ rpmbuild --recompile --noclean ./SRPMS/somerpm.src.rpm

Now one can change spec, src and rebuild RPM or SRPM.

To build RPM for 386 on x86_64 machine

rpmbuild -bb --noclean --target=i686-redhat-linux SPECS/pcsc-lite.spec
